What's New

  • New School Canteen

    Every day we have new meals. We have options from one dahl, roti/rice, a vegetable and desert. We sometimes get french fries, hakka noodles, munchurin and custard (all pre-covid 19). Now students bring there own lunches and eat in the classrooms.

  • Implemented Online Attendance Management System

    1. Attendance Even though this option isn't avalible to students, whenever a student joins the class the teachers are immediatly notified, through a teams feature. If students don't attend three classes out of six, they are marked absent for the whole day.
    2. No changing tabs! Changing tabs is something many students have been doing. Due to this, the school has created there own school account from which a google tab can be opened. Since for many students this is the only google tab, so the school knows exactly what they are doing.
    3. Breaks Due to the Covid-19 pandemic, all schools have had to shift to online learning. This causes students eyes to hurt. This is why are school offers a 15 minute break after the first two classes, a ten minute one after the next two, and a five minute after the two classes after the ten minute break.
